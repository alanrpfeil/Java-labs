CSE21 : Lab#9 - Inheritance

Overview

We will use examples given in the lecture to extend the behavior of basic Counters. This lab is meant to familiarize you with the usefulness and concepts of class inheritance. It means traits and behaviors of the parents/super classes will be passed down to the children.
